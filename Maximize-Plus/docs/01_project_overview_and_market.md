## 1.2 Glossary of Terms
Standardized fintech and platform terminology used throughout the documentation suite.

| Abbreviation | Full Form | Definition |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Stacking Engine** | Algorithmic Stacking Engine | Proprietary core calculation engine that simultaneously applies Gift Card discounts, Affiliate Cashback, Brand Coupons, and Card/Bank offers to minimize effective order cost |
| **MaxCoin** | Maximize Reward Coin | Non-expiring platform rewards currency pegged 1:1 to INR (₹1), earned on transactions and redeemable for gift cards or loyalty conversions |
| **PPI** | Prepaid Payment Instrument | RBI-regulated financial instruments (wallets, gift cards, vouchers) facilitating purchase of goods and services against value stored |
| **EPC** | Earnings Per Click | Average affiliate revenue generated by the platform per outgoing user redirect to a merchant |
| **Affiliate Rake** | Commission Rake | The percentage commission paid by affiliate networks (Admitad, Cuelinks, Impact) to Maximize-Plus for driving a verified sale |
| **Smart Overlay** | Shopping Assistant Overlay | Mobile accessibility overlay or desktop extension widget that automatically wakes up on merchant checkouts to suggest cheaper stacked payment combinations |
| **Maharaja Points** | Air India Maharaja Club Points | Loyalty currency of Air India, convertible from MaxCoins at a 5:4 ratio (5 MaxCoins = 4 Maharaja Points) |
| **KYC** | Know Your Customer | Mandatory Indian/Global identity verification process required before processing wallet withdrawals exceeding regulatory thresholds |
| **GMV** | Gross Merchandise Value | Total gross monetary value of all gift cards sold and affiliate carts checked out through the platform |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost | Blended marketing expenditure required to onboard one active purchasing user |
| **LTV** | Customer Lifetime Value | Net margin contribution expected from a shopper across their active platform lifespan |
| **PG** | Payment Gateway | Financial intermediary (Razorpay, Juspay, Cashfree) processing user card, UPI, and netbanking transactions |
| **MCC** | Merchant Category Code | Four-digit classification code used by credit card issuers to categorize transaction types for bonus reward points |

## 1.3 Executive Summary & Vision Statement
**Maximize-Plus** is an enterprise-grade fintech savings-stacking and rewards platform modeled after **Maximize.money**. Operating at the intersection of affiliate aggregation, digital prepaid instruments, and smart payment routing, the platform solves the fragmentation of online consumer savings.

**The Vision:** To build the ultimate consumer financial layer where every online purchase automatically executes at the mathematical minimum price through algorithmic deal stacking, converting everyday spend into permanent, non-expiring wealth via MaxCoins.

## 1.8 Revenue Model & Unit Economics

### Platform Revenue Streams
1.  **Affiliate Commission Rake (Primary):** Maximize-Plus earns a 3% to 12% commission from affiliate networks (Admitad, Cuelinks) on verified sales. The platform passes ~60-80% of this value to the user as discounts/MaxCoins and retains 20-40% as net revenue margin.
2.  **Gift Card Float Margin:** Bulk purchasing of brand vouchers at wholesale discount (e.g., 8% off MRP) and retailing to consumers at stacked retail discount (e.g., 5% off MRP), capturing a 3% spread.
3.  **Brand Sponsored Stack Placements:** Brands pay a promotional fee (`placement_fee`) to feature their vouchers or exclusive coupons at the top of the Smart Stacking Calculator recommendations.

### Unit Economics Example (₹5,000 Adidas Order)
*   **Gross Order Value (MRP):** ₹5,000
*   **Gift Card Wholesale Discount (10%):** -₹500 (Platform wholesale cost: ₹4,500)
*   **Consumer Gift Card Discount (4%):** -₹200 (Consumer pays ₹4,800 for GC) -> *Platform Spread Margin: +₹300*
*   **Affiliate Network Commission (8% of ₹4,800):** +₹384 paid by brand to Maximize-Plus
*   **Consumer Affiliate Cashback Passed (5%):** -₹240 passed to consumer balance -> *Platform Affiliate Margin: +₹144*
*   **Brand Coupon Code (SAVE10 - 10%):** -₹500 (Funded entirely by Adidas)
*   **Bank Offer (HDFC Card 5%):** -₹225 (Funded entirely by HDFC Bank)
*   **Net Consumer Payment:** ₹3,835 (*Consumer total savings: ₹1,165 / 23.3%*)
*   **Net Platform Gross Margin per Order:** ₹300 (GC Spread) + ₹144 (Affiliate Spread) = **₹444 net margin (8.88% rake)**

### Phase 2: The Miles & Travel Expansion (Months 3-4)
*   **Target Audience:** Frequent corporate travelers and luxury hotel hobbyists.
*   **Hook:** Exclusive transfer bonus campaign: *"Convert MaxCoins to Air India Maharaja Points at 1:1 ratio for 7 days."*

*   **Viral Trigger:** Recipients opening the shared cart see: *"Rahul saved ₹1,100 on this cart using Maximize-Plus. Claim your ₹100 welcome MaxCoins to buy this cart now."*
*   **Result:** Organic peer-to-peer customer acquisition lowering blended CAC below ₹80.

## 1.11 Compliance & Regulatory Framework

### Reserve Bank of India (RBI) PPI Guidelines
*   Gift cards retailed via Maximize-Plus are closed-system or semi-closed PPIs issued by authorized banking/non-banking entities.
*   Maximum value per individual closed gift voucher is capped at ₹10,000 per transaction pursuant to master directions.

### Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023
*   Explicit, unbundled user consent required before capturing transaction SMS notifications or inspecting merchant checkout URLs via mobile overlay.
*   Full data localization enforced: all Aurora PostgreSQL and Redis databases hosted inside AWS `ap-south-1` (Mumbai).

### PCI-DSS v4.0 Payment Security
*   No direct storage of raw credit/debit card primary account numbers (PAN).
*   All saved payment instruments routed via RBI-mandated Card-on-File (CoF) network tokenization vaults (Juspay Safe).

*   **Monthly Platform Fixed Cost:** ₹29,59,453 (HR + OpEx)

Once platform active purchasing users exceed **15,000 monthly active transactors**, the business achieves net operational profitability.
